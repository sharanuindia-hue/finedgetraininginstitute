Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30/08/2025

Website: www.finedgetraininginstitute.com

Business Name: FinEdge Training Institute

At FinEdge Training Institute, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website, training services, and placement assistance programs.

1. Information We Collect

- Personal Details: Name, email, phone number, address.
- Payment Details: Billing information, transaction IDs (processed securely via third-party gateways).
- Training Data: Course progress, assessments, and participation history.
- Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

- Providing and improving training programs.
- Sending course updates, placement-related communication, and important notices.
- Processing payments securely.
- Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Sharing

- We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
- We may share data only with:
• Payment processors (for transactions).
• Placement partners or employers (for career opportunities, only with your consent).
• Legal authorities, if required by law.

4. Data Security

- We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your information.
- Payments are processed via secure, PCI-DSS-compliant gateways.
- However, no online transmission can be guaranteed as 100% secure. Users share information at their own risk.

5. Cookies & Tracking

- Our website may use cookies to improve user experience.
- You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly.

6. User Rights

- Access your personal data.
- Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data.
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
To exercise these rights, email us at info@finedgetraininginstitute.com.

7. Data Retention

- We retain your data only as long as necessary for training, placement, or legal compliance.
- Once data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

8. Children’s Privacy

- Our services are intended for individuals 18 years and above.
- If a minor enrolls, it must be with parent/guardian consent.

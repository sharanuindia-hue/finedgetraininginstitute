About Us

Finedge Training Institute

Finedge Training Institute is an NSDC–affiliated training partner of the BFSI Sector Skill Council of India (BFSI SSC), focused on making graduates job-ready for careers in Banking, Financial Services and Insurance. We bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world employability through structured online training, live sessions and dedicated placement support.

Our programs are designed around actual BFSI job roles – combining product knowledge, operations, sales & service skills, compliance awareness and professional communication.

The BFSI world is being reshaped by digital innovation, FinTech, and data analytics. Organizations now demand professionals who combine business acumen with digital intelligence.

Finedge Training Institute provides future-ready training designed for this pace of transformation. Our programs integrate financial expertise with digital proficiency, giving you the knowledge and tools to drive growth, innovation, and a successful career in the modern financial ecosystem.

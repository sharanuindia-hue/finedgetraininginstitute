BFSI Career Accelerator ‚Äì 30 Days to Your First BFSI Job

The BFSI Career Accelerator Program is a 30-day, 100% online, job-oriented program designed to prepare fresh graduates and early-career professionals for entry-level roles across Banking, NBFCs, Insurance, Mutual Funds and Fintech.

Delivered through live evening classes, the program bridges the gap between academic knowledge and real-world BFSI requirements by combining core domain concepts, practical case studies, role plays and interview preparation.

As an Authorised Training Partner of BFSI SSC ‚Äì NSDC, Government of India, Finedge Training Institute offers co-branded certification and focused placement assistance with BFSI recruiters.

Who is this program for?

  • Fresh graduates (any stream) looking for a first job in BFSI
  • Candidates from non-finance backgrounds who want to enter banking & financial services
  • Early-career professionals seeking a structured career shift into BFSI roles

Program Structure ‚Äì What You Will Learn

The curriculum is aligned to industry requirements and focuses on practical skills needed in day-to-day banking and financial services roles.

Module 1 ‚Äì Banking Fundamentals
  • Overview of Retail, Corporate & Digital Banking
  • Types of bank accounts, deposits and lending products
  • Branch operations & customer service basics
Module 2 ‚Äì NBFC & Insurance Basics
  • Difference between banks and NBFCs
  • Loan products (home, auto, personal, business loans)
  • Life & general insurance concepts and sales process
Module 3 ‚Äì Mutual Funds & Wealth Management
  • Introduction to mutual funds, SIPs and investment products
  • Basics of investor profiling and suitability
  • Cross-sell and upsell opportunities in BFSI
Module 4 ‚Äì Credit, Collections & Risk
  • Loan lifecycle ‚Äì sourcing, documentation and disbursal
  • Credit checks, underwriting support & field verification
  • Basics of collections, recovery and customer communication
Module 5 ‚Äì Operations, KYC & Compliance
  • KYC, AML and regulatory requirements
  • Back-office processing and documentation
  • Operational risk awareness in BFSI roles
Module 6 ‚Äì Job Readiness & Placement Support
  • Resume building & LinkedIn optimisation for BFSI roles
  • Mock interviews with HR & line managers
  • Interview etiquette, communication and body language

Key Outcomes

  • Clear understanding of core BFSI products, processes and customer handling
  • Improved confidence for interviews through mock sessions and feedback
  • Co-branded certificate from Finedge Training Institute & BFSI SSC*
  • Placement assistance with partner banks, NBFCs and financial institutions
